Cloudiness

"Cloudiness" is likely a somewhat arbitrary number, as there are major "bins" in the data, with many dots in a horizontal lines other than 0% (perfectly clear skis) and 100% (totally cloudy). I would expect it to be a continuous measurement betweeen 0% and 100%. This data is not particularly useful given the "binning".
